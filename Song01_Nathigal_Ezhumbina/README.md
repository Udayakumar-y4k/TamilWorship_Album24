Psalm 93 – Short but powerful declaration: The Lord reigns! Even when floods rise and roar, God’s throne is established from everlasting and His holiness fills the temple.
Closing Prayer
Lord Jesus Christ,
You reign above every flood and storm.
When the waters rise and roar against us,
You remain unshaken and glorious.
Help us trust in Your eternal reign.
