Psalm 97 – Celebration of God’s reign, His holiness, and the judgment of evil. Calls all the earth to rejoice and turn from idols to the Holy One.
Closing Prayer
Lord Jesus Christ,
You are holy.
Help us remember Your holiness and turn from all evil.
Let us worship You in spirit and truth.
Burn away everything unclean in us.
